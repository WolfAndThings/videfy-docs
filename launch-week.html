Launch Week.
Every channel. We voice. Always.

All posts go through company accounts. Personal LinkedIn stays clean. X allows "I" + "we" mix. Every channel has its own personality. We default to "we" everywhere else — it's a team.

Channel personalities

Each channel has a distinct voice

Lock these voices Mon. Don't drift. Each platform reads different — same brand, different volume.

TikTok

Punchy. Hook-first.

"We shipped this." POV + bold thesis + BTS. First 1.5s = hook. No fluff. Casual.

Instagram

Visual flex. Save-worthy.

Carousels that solve a problem. Reels that look intentional. We design for the bookmark, not the like.

LinkedIn

Brand-led. Thoughtful.

Company page only. Industry thesis + ROI math + agency case studies. We sound smart, not corporate.

X (Twitter)

Build in public. I + We mix.

"I" for opinions / takes / personality · "we" when talking company / shipping / numbers. Numbers + screenshots. Threads Wed.

YouTube Shorts

Recycle the wins.

Top TikTok wins re-cut for YT. Long-tail SEO compounds. Pin best to channel.

Discord

Warm. Team voice.

"We" not "I." Founder presence + 5 personality bots (Curator · Coach · Critic · Connector · Brainstorm).

Email

Direct. Behind scenes.

Founder signature ok (private channel). Numbers + transparency + one CTA. Ship Tuesdays.
