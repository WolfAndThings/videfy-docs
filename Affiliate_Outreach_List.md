## Realistic outcome

- 50 cold DMs → ~30% reply rate = 15 conversations
- 15 conversations → ~50% commit = 7-8 affiliates
- Of those, 3-5 actually post within first 30 days
- Of those, 1-2 drive meaningful traffic

**Day 0 partner-creator commitment target: 5 minimum.**

## Outreach DM principles

- Personalize first line with specific recent post (don't copy-paste)
- Lead with what they get, not what we want
- One ask: "want early access?" not "want to be an affiliate?"
- Time-bound: "launching in [X weeks]"
- Send a free demo asset they can use immediately
- Don't pitch the product on DM #1 — DM #2 once they reply

## Tracking

- Airtable column: Outreach Status (cold / warm / committed / active / lapsed)
- Per-affiliate: signups generated, content posted count, last activity date
- Weekly review: drop affiliates inactive 30+ days
