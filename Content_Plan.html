03 · Per platform

Channel plans

TikTok

Lead acquisition · 3–4 reels/wk

Audience: Creators, freelance editors, marketers (18–34). Top of funnel.

Mix: 40% hook+demo, 25% bold thesis, 20% BTS workflow, 15% Avatar output flex.

Lead hooks: POV + cost reframe + tool-stack reveal. First 1.5s = hook. 15–45s sweet spot.

Day-90 floor: 8–25K followers, 5–10 posts hit 100K+ views.

Instagram

Visual archive · 4 reels + 1–2 carousels/wk

Audience: Creators, designers, in-house creative teams (25–44).

Mix: Cross-post TT wins (re-edited) + 23 carousel design skeletons from bank + daily Stories.

Carousel topics: Old workflow vs Videfy · 5 reasons your videos take 6 weeks · $80K agency vs $59 Videfy seat.

Day-90 floor: 5–15K followers. Saves > reels for IG creator audience.

LinkedIn

B2B conversion · 3 posts + 1 long-form/wk

Audience: Agency CDs, in-house marketers, CMOs. Highest LTV channel ($300–1500/mo accounts).

Mix: 30% case study, 25% thesis, 20% ROI breakdown, 15% tool stack reveal, 10% hot take.

Wednesday lock: Long-form weekly. 5–10 strategic comments/day on agency leaders.

Day-90 floor: 2–6K followers, 50–150 high-LTV trial signups.

X (Twitter)

Founder voice · 1–2 threads + daily replies

Audience: Devs, builders, AI/SaaS founders, press, investors.

Mix: 50% build-in-public, 30% Avenue-1 (Claude MCP) demos, 20% replies to AI/video accounts.

Wednesday thread = 3× engagement. "I spent $X on Y before figuring out Z" formula. 1 calculated provocation/month.

Day-90 floor: 3–10K followers, dev/investor attention.

YouTube Shorts

Cross-post · 3/wk

Audience: Same as TikTok but slightly older. Shorts trend toward longer watch time.

Mix: Recycle TikTok wins. Pin top-performing as profile featured.

Day-90 floor: 1–3K subs. Long-tail SEO benefit grows year-over-year.

Email · Newsletter

Starts Wk 4 · Tuesday weekly

Wk 1–3: Build email list only. Lead magnets live, ManyChat capturing. No sends yet.

Wk 4+: First newsletter ships Tue. Cadence locks weekly.

Sections: Hook · Wins/numbers · Build log · Featured affiliate · Tip · CTA.

Day-90 floor: 1K–3K subs. Highest-conversion channel by margin (5–10× social).

06 · Conversion flow

ManyChat — comment to DM to email

Reels with comment-trigger CTAs convert at 5–15× higher than bio-link. ManyChat auto-DMs commenters → captures email → drops lead magnet. Bring in by Wk 4.

When to add

Wk 4. Need 2–3 reels averaging 50K+ views first. ManyChat without organic traction wastes the integration.

Trigger keywords

"PROMPTS" — drops 30 hooks PDF
"SKELETONS" — drops carousel Figma
"WORKFLOW" — drops 8-tool stack PDF
"GENERATE" — drops 2,500 credits

Auto-flow

Comment → ManyChat DMs → "Drop your email" → Beehiiv subscribe → tag in CRM by trigger word → trial nurture sequence.

Setup cost: ManyChat Pro $15/mo + ConvertKit/Beehiiv free tier. Maintenance: 1 hr/month. ROI: typical IG creator using ManyChat doubles email capture per viral reel.

07 · Team machine

Production line — 2-person team

Two people producing. Both full-time — 40–50 hrs/wk each, 80–100 hrs combined. Sustainable for 6 months only with batching + AI assist + 2 days off/week.

Role split

Alex · Strategy + Written + On-camera

Hat 1 — Strategy + Systems: Airtable, ManyChat flows, affiliate ops, KPIs, planning, press. ~10–13 hrs/wk.

Hat 2 — Written: LinkedIn long-form, X thread, newsletter, carousel copy, all reel scripts. ~17–22 hrs/wk.

Hat 3 — On-camera: 3-4 reels/wk + Avatar + podcast + hero film. ~5–7 hrs/wk (batch Mon + Thu).

Total: 40–50 hrs/wk · full-time +.

Co · Production + Ops

Owns: all video editing, cross-platform re-cuts, carousel design, scheduling via Blotato, TT/IG community, ManyChat ops support, screen-record demos, podcast appearances when on-camera.

Output: 3-4 edited originals + 3-4 cross-post adapts + 1 carousel + daily TT/IG community.

Total: 40–50 hrs/wk · full-time.

One Mon shoot block → 8 outputs by Friday

Alex performs 2 reels Mon morning (raw 4–6 min total) → Co cuts 3 TikTok versions → Co adapts IG Reels + YouTube Shorts → Alex builds X thread from transcript → Alex reframes as LinkedIn post → Alex pulls Email newsletter section → Alex writes IG carousel copy → Co designs.

Result: 1 shoot block (90 min Alex + 6-8 hrs Co) = 8 outputs across 5 platforms by Friday.

How to keep this sustainable
  • Batch shoot Mon + Thu. 90 min shoots → 4-5 reels each block.
  • AI first drafts. ChatGPT/Videfy for newsletter + LI long-form (Alex edits) — saves ~40% writing time.
  • Templates over fresh design. 23 carousel skeletons → swap copy + image, don't redesign.
  • Reply caps. 30 min morning + 30 min evening. Not infinite.
  • Two days off/week. Non-negotiable.
  • Cut signal: if either hits 50+ hrs/wk for 2 weeks, drop to 2 reels/wk.
