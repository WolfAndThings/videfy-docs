## 🎯 Top viral patterns (use these)

From our 312K-engagement data:
1. **Cost reframe** — "Your agency charges $X. We ship it for $Y."
2. **Process denial trio** — "Didn't film. Didn't book talent. Didn't open Premiere."
3. **Conditional shame** — "If your X still does Y, you don't have a Z."
4. **Era-end** — "It is officially over."
5. **Tool callout** — "Everyone's still using X — but [problem]"
6. **Stop-start** — "Stop X. Start Y."

Applied across Launch Week:
- Day 1: Era-end ("Production companies have 24 hours left")
- Day 2: Cost reframe ("$80K → $59/mo")
- Day 3: Process denial trio
- Day 4: Stop-start
- Day 5: Cost reframe (47 minutes)
- Day 6: Tool callout shame

## ✅ Locked rules

### Voice
- LinkedIn = brand-only "we" · NEVER "I"
- X = "I" + "we" mix (I for opinions · we for company shipping)
- TT/IG/YT/Discord/Email = "we" team voice
- Discord/Email founder voice OK (private channels)

### Visual
- Faceless production default · Avatar / screen-record / b-roll
- Alex on camera when needed · Co handles all editing/design/scheduling
- Personal LinkedIn stays clean · all posts via @videfy company accounts

### CTA
- Single word `STUDIO` across all public posts (Wk 1)
- Manual DM replies Wk 1 · ManyChat goes live Wk 2

### Cadence (2-person team)
- 10-14 outputs/wk
- 60-70 hrs combined (Alex 20 · Co 40-50)
- Batch shoot Mon + Thu
- 2 days off non-negotiable
