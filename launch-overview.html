Test. Learn. Build organic.

2-person team. No paid ads at start. Content engine first. Layers added once base is real. Sustained launch beats one-shot bomb. This is hard. Most launches don't hit. Numbers in this doc are floors, not promises.

Operating principle

Test → Learn → Build organic. Wk 1-3 is content cadence only. Wk 4+ layers in newsletter, ManyChat, affiliate. Wk 5-6 PH launch. Each new layer waits for the previous one to prove out. No premature optimization. No layer added before its prerequisite is real.

02 · Honest Floor

Reality check — this is hard

The floor

A real AI creator (anonymized — our reality anchor) shipped 4.8 posts/wk over 11 months to median 22K views. Top single posts hit 64K likes / 800K views. That's the realistic ceiling for a year of disciplined team content with no pre-existing audience.

Most launches don't hit

~70% of cold-start launches stall under 5K followers in 90 days. ~30% chance of one viral hit (1M+) in first 6 months. Trial-to-paid conversion is 1–3% industry standard, not 10%. Need 14-day Pro trial discipline.

Team capacity

Realistic: ~18–22 outputs/wk with batching + AI assist. 80–100 hrs/wk combined — both Alex and Co full-time, 40-50 hrs each. Burnout protection = 2 days off/week. One viral hit per 30–50 posts is the realistic ratio.

05 · Pre-mortem

Why this can fail

Founder burnout

Both at 40-50 hrs/wk for 6 months is real risk. Mitigation: 2 days off/week, batch shooting, drop reply volume past Wk 8.

Free trial conversion

No free tier in v1. Industry trial→paid is 1–3%. If we hit floor (1%), MRR projections cut in half. Watch closely Wk 4–8.

Bomb fizzle

Day-0 hero film could land at 100K views instead of 5M. Plan B: ongoing daily content compounds anyway. Bomb is bonus, not load-bearing.

No viral hit

~30% chance of zero 1M+ post in first 6 months. If that happens, MRR floor is $20–40K not $60–150K. Affiliate program + agency conversion become primary path.

Account flag

Cold accounts at high cadence = throttling risk. Mitigation: warmup phase Wk -6 to -1, watch view counts daily, slow down if shadowbanned.

Voice drift

Without daily anchor, brand voice fragments by Wk 8. Mitigation: voice lock doc, founder reviews everything Mo 1–3, banned-words list active.
