# Videfy Cold-Start Rollout
## Zero followers → real numbers, real timeline

Calibrated to actual recent AI/SaaS launches. No hopium.

**Honest baseline for Videfy (no founder pre-following):**
- 10K followers: **12-16 weeks**, not 6
- First 1M-view post: **week 6-10** if hook discipline holds
- First $100K MRR: **month 6-9** (not 8 weeks)
- First viral might never come — plan for compounding, not lottery

## Stage 0 — Foundation (Week -1)
Lock everything before posting. Late changes break momentum.

1. **Voice lock** — 3 anchor phrases. "Production layer" / "studio of one" / "without the chaos."
2. **Visual lock** — single editorial direction. No drift.
3. **Founder face decision** — Cluely won by founder face + provocation. Granola won faceless via writing. Pick lane.
4. **Product readiness** — landing page lives, sign-up flow tested, free trial works. Don't drive traffic to friction.
5. **Account warmup** — post 3-5 low-stakes reels Week -1 to thaw the algorithm. Cold accounts get throttled hard at sudden 7/wk cadence.
6. **Pixel + analytics** — Meta pixel on landing, attribution tagged per channel.

---

## Stage 1 — Cold Start (Weeks 1-4)
Goal: **first 1K followers per platform.** 1M views unlikely yet.

1. **One platform leads.** Pick TikTok OR X (not both). TikTok = creator audience. X = founder/dev audience. Mirror to others.
2. **Cadence: 1-2 reels/day on lead, 4/wk on mirrors.** Lower than industry hype claims, but sustainable.
3. **Hook discipline.** First 30 posts test 6-8 hook patterns. Cut what doesn't beat 5K views in 24h.
4. **No CTAs Week 1-2.** Build pattern recognition. Add bio link week 3.
5. **Founder DMs 5 creators/day.** Comment, DM, no pitch. Just network density.
6. **Realistic week-1 numbers:** 200-2,000 views/post, 0-50 followers/day. If you get 10K views, you got lucky. If you get 100K, scale immediately.

**Realistic Week-4 outcome:** 800-2,500 followers TikTok, 200-800 IG, 100-400 LinkedIn.

---

## Stage 2 — Audience Build (Weeks 5-12)
Goal: **5K-15K followers across stack, first compounding wins.**

1. **Cadence: 7-10 reels/week** on lead, 4/week mirrors. This is the cluely cadence. Cut if it kills quality.
2. **Mix ratio:** 50% identity-flip (broad), 30% pain-validation, 20% capability demo.
3. **First carousel** — Wk 6 IG + LinkedIn. Use design bank.
4. **Email capture goes live** — Wk 6. Lead magnet = "8-tool video stack → one project" PDF (use ohneis aesthetic).
5. **First viral attempt** — Wk 8-10 plant 1 calculated bomb (provocative hook, founder face, hot take). Most don't hit. One in five do.
6. **Realistic Week-12 outcome:** 8K-15K TT, 3K-8K IG, 2K-5K LinkedIn, 200-500 email subs.

---

## Stage 3 — Conversion Engine (Weeks 13-20)
Goal: **first 50-200 paying users.** Not 5K signups.

1. **Demo content unlocks.** Show real Videfy workflow. Screen-record + voice-over.
2. **Founder Q&A weekly.** Surfaces real objections. Reuse for sales pages.
3. **Product Hunt launch — Week 14.** Need 50+ committed upvoters lined up before. Real outcome: 200-2,000 trial signups in 24h, 20-150 paid conversions.
4. **First case study by Wk 16.** Real customer testimonial. 1 polished version → 4 cuts (TT, IG, LI, X).
5. **Pricing reveal post** — Wk 17. Compare $80K agency vs Videfy seat.
6. **Realistic Wk-20 outcome:** 50-200 paying users, $5K-20K MRR (depends on pricing tier).

---

## Stage 4 — Category Push (Weeks 21-28)
Goal: **$50-100K MRR.** Recognized voice in AI video.

1. **Manifesto drop** — Week 22. 3-min founder thesis. Owns "production layer" phrase.
2. **Newsletter launch** — Week 22. Tuesday weekly. Aim 1K subs by Wk 28.
3. **Press push** — Week 24. 10 outbound: AI Breakdown, Lenny's, This Week in Startups, Marketing Brew, Hacker News.
4. **Affiliate program** — Week 24. 10 vetted creators get free seats + 20% rev share.
5. **First paid amplification** — Week 26. $3K/mo Meta on top-3 organic posts. Watch CAC vs LTV. Kill if CAC > $200 for $30/mo product.
6. **Realistic Wk-28 outcome:** 30K-80K total followers, $50K-100K MRR.

---

## Stage 5 — Scale (Months 7-12)
Goal: **$250K-500K MRR.**

1. Paid spend ramps — $10-30K/mo.
2. Agency partnership program — tiered.
3. Founder on 8-12 podcasts.
4. Conference: 1 keynote slot (booth not necessary).
5. Annual report drop — "State of AI Video Production" Q4.

## Honest Risks (with mitigation)

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| First 4 weeks = 0 traction | 70% | Plan for it. Don't quit cadence. Cluely posted 30 duds before viral. |
| Account shadow-ban from cadence | 40% | Warmup phase + watch view counts daily |
| Voice drift across team posts | 60% | Style guide locked Wk -1, founder reviews everything Mo 1-3 |
| First viral never comes | 30% | Compound followers > viral lottery. 7 mid-posts beats 1 spike |
| Trial → paid <2% | 50% | Product onboarding is the problem, not content. Fix landing page first |
| Content fatigue Mo 3 | 80% | Production system feeds 30+ new hooks/month or it dies |
| Free tier cannibalizes paid | 30% | Tier limits visible upfront; soft paywall on outputs not on access |

## What This Plan Is Not
- Not a "go viral and win" bet. The comps say compounding follower count + product readiness wins.
- Not 8 weeks to $1.5M. Realistic is **6-9 months to first $100K MRR.**
- Not a content-only play. Product Hunt + Press + Partnerships are 30-50% of pipeline.
