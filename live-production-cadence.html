Phase 01 · pre-live

Lead-up · 7 days
down to T-minus zero.

Most live failures happen here · before anyone hits the broadcast button. Walk this list every cycle. Same rhythm every time.

7d
T-minus 7 days · announce
Title locked (Alex + Doron). Project locked (Vaughn picks the build). Outline drafted (Alex). Announcement posts go out: LinkedIn (company), X, IG, beta email list. Discord invite link in every post. Pin announcement in Discord #announcements. Hubspot form active on landing page.
3d
T-minus 3 days · prep + remind
10 Q&A seed questions drafted (Alex + Doron, with routing notes). Reminder email goes out. Story countdown begins (daily). All 4 team members confirm availability. Vaughn does solo run-through of the build (private). Marta confirms platform is in good shape (no breaking bugs).
24h
T-minus 24 hours · final reminders
24-hr reminder email. Repost on X + LinkedIn. Story countdown final push. Discord announcement: "Live tomorrow at [time]."
2h
T-minus 2 hours · personal prep
Eat. Hydrate. Quiet space. Outline pulled up on phone or 2nd device. Background prepped (clean wall or virtual). Notifications killed across all devices.
T-minus 1 hour · the day-of block (full team)

Four 15-minute blocks. Live Ops calls each transition. No skipping.

①
Setup
15 min
Rooms ready. Lighting set. Audio plugged. Phones silent. Outline on 2nd screen.
②
Test
15 min
Zoom screen share working. Loom backup capturing the right screen. Audio levels good across all 4 mics.
③
Rehearsal
15 min
Vaughn walks first 5 min of build. Alex coaches on-cam direction. Q&A routing confirmed live.
④
Dry run
15 min
Full hand-off flow: intros to CTA close. Anyone fumbles, run it again. 5-min buffer at the end.
T-minus 5 min · soft launch
Everyone in Zoom room with cams off. "Starting soon" card up. Music bed running quietly. Audience joins, counter starts climbing. Live Ops watching join flow.
Phase 02 · the live

Sixty minutes.
Five segments.

Open · walkthrough · transition · Q&A · close. Time anchors are guides, not handcuffs. If chat is hot, stay in Q&A. If walkthrough drags, cut it short. The cadence is the floor, not the ceiling.

0
0:00 to 0:05 · Open
Cameras on. Alex opens with warm greeting + 60-sec "who we are" trust block. Founding team = ex-Google · domain experts from the pain. Each person delivers their oneliner. Title of live + what audience will get + when CTA lands.
5
0:05 to 0:30 · Walkthrough
Vaughn takes the screen. Narrates the build. Six beats: Setup project · Drop in raw assets · AI inside the flow · Scene-level edit · Loop in collab/client review · Export + ship. Doron drops domain context where it lands naturally. Alex calls first CTA at 0:05, second at 0:25. Live Ops watches chat, feeds top 2-3 questions to Alex via backchannel.
30
0:30 to 0:35 · Transition
Vaughn hands off to Alex. Alex teases Q&A: "Drop questions in chat now." Quick recap of what just got built (30 sec). Open the floor.
35
0:35 to 0:55 · Q&A
Alex reads questions aloud, routes by topic. Platform questions to Vaughn or Marta (if on call). Industry/strategy to Doron. Roadmap to Alex. Drops 5-8 of the seeded questions if chat is quiet. Live Ops keeps surfacing the best ones, flags trolls, mutes if needed. Energy check at 0:50, wrap up if dragging.
55
0:55 to 1:00 · Close
Alex calls last CTA: "videfy.com/studio · use keyword STUDIO in chat." Discord invite link drops in chat. Thanks the team by name + thanks the audience. "Replay goes up tomorrow · next live in 2 weeks · same time." End broadcast.
T-plus 5 minutes

Debrief.
Win + lesson per person.

Team stays in Zoom (cams off) for 5 minutes after broadcast ends. Quick capture while it's fresh. No long retros · run them weekly, not per-live.

Debrief script · ~5 min total
1. Each person: one win + one lesson (1 min each). 2. Live Ops confirms Loom + Zoom recording uploaded clean. 3. Anything broken on the platform · Marta logs it for engineering. 4. Hand off to Phase 03.

Standing rules

Notes that
apply every cycle.

●
Always live · never pre-recorded
Trust signal
Real-time Q&A is what earns trust. No edited fake-live. No simulated chat. Pre-recorded video lives in repurpose, not the live slot.
●
Rough cut is fine
For now
Webcam + AirPods + window light. Mics upgrade after live #1. Pro standards on prep, not pro-grade gear.
●
One tier per live
No mixing
Don't blend audiences. Title promises the tier. Mixing tiers = nobody is the right audience.
●
Backchannel for tech
Silent fixes
Team-only Discord thread for live coordination. Never solve audio drops on stream. Solve silently in the backchannel.
CTA discipline
CTA at minute 5, 25, and close. Three is the floor. More is fine if natural. Discord is where the community lives between lives · drive every viewer there at the close.
