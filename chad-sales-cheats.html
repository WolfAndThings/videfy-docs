3 scripts to memorize.

Script 01 · 3-question opener
"This call is not about me. This call is about you. My agenda is not important. If we walked away today and I didn't answer the three questions you came with, it would be a total waste of your time. What are those three questions?"
When: Mtg 1 open · within first 90 seconds
Script 02 · Commission breath
"Hey, I've got a couple kids. Do I work on commission? Yes. Do I want to sell you as much stuff as possible? That'd be great. But that stuff's not important to you. I'm not going to bring it up again."
When: Mtg 1 · before anything that smells like pitch. Removes adversarial energy.
Script 03 · Pipeline disqualifier (internal)
"Not responding. Can't reach decider. No compelling event. Budget unconfirmed. Pain not quantified. → Cut."
When: weekly pipeline review · ruthless. 5 red flags = dead deal.
12 sales rules

Habits over hacks.

01Always Be Discovering · not closing
Top closers 70% questions / 30% solution. Bottom reps flip it. Discovery never ends.
025-minute prep · not 40
Company site (2m) · LinkedIn (1m) · Google news (1m) · ChatGPT for 10-Ks (1m). Come w/ perspective, not interrogation.
033 to 5 deals max
20 × 10% = 2 deals of effort. 5 × 100% = 5 deals of effort. "Organized procrastination" kills quota.
04Disqualify fast
23 hours wasted last yr on 3 deals that should've died at Mtg 1. Speed to no = same value as speed to yes.
05Silence is strategy
Top reps talk 46%. Bottom 72%. (1M+ call data.) Ask. Shut up. Awkward pause = where deals are won.
062-hour response time
30-day closes avg 4hr response. 6-mo draggers avg 2 days. Urgency contagious. Speed = respect, not desperation.
07Never "just following up"
Every email = ONE of: new info · specific question · clear next step. No third option. Don't send if none.
08Voicemail · 2 then stop
VM1 = 15 sec context. VM2 = 30 sec context + social proof. Goal = email reply, not callback. 115% email lift.
09Independently wealthy mindset
Huge pipeline = leverage. When you don't need any single deal, you get the deal. Need repels.
10Rhythm beats motivation
Monday: pipeline review · top 3 deals · 2 hrs outreach. Same. Every. Week. Motivation = feeling. Rhythm = system.
11Give first
Their problem. Their world. Your quota isn't theirs. Trust → buy. Make-it-about-you = lost.
12Ask about prior history
Chad lost $65K once because he never asked "have you worked with us before?" Their IT had bad experience 2 yrs prior. One missing question = $65K.

Rapport > small talk

Open Mtg 1.
Right way vs wrong way.

Small talk (don't)Rapport (do)
"How's the weather?""What made you take this call?"
"Big day ahead?""What's the most important thing you want out of this 30 minutes?"
"How was your weekend?""What got you looking at this category in the first place?"
"Where are you dialing from?""Tell me what's broken right now that made this call worth scheduling."
Small talk makes YOU comfortable. Rapport makes THEM feel heard. One earns the next 25 minutes. The other wastes 5.
