# Videfy — Influencer + Community Outreach Phases

**Goal:** Build relationships and touch points across multiple channels per person. Multiple touch points = remembered. Single touch = forgotten.

**Principle:** Warm > cold. Slow > fast. Personal > templated. Multiple touch points across LinkedIn / IG / X / podcast / DM compound.

---

## The "touch point" theory

Every target person needs **3-5 touch points** before they say yes. Average is 7. Each platform is one touch.

Touch points:
1. Comment on their post (organic reply)
2. DM after 2-3 comment exchanges
3. Tag them or mention them in a piece of your content
4. Email them after they've seen your name a few times
5. Show up in their podcast / live / event
6. Direct ask (after warming above)

Hit them on **at least 3 channels** before the ask.

## Phases — slow burn to high-touch

### Phase 1 (Wk 1-4) — Warm Up
**Goal:** Be visible, not pushy. Get name recognition.

- [ ] Identify 50 target accounts across IG / X / LinkedIn (mix of Tier 1-4 from Affiliate List)
- [ ] Comment on their content 3-5x/wk for first month — substantive, not "great post!"
- [ ] Repost / quote-tweet their wins with thoughtful add-ons
- [ ] Show up in their live streams / Spaces if relevant
- [ ] No DMs yet · pure visibility

### Phase 2 (Wk 5-8) — First Conversations
**Goal:** Move from anonymous comment → recognized DM.

- [ ] After 4+ weeks of comments, send first DM:
  > "Hey [name] — been following your work on [specific topic]. Just launched something I think you'd like. Free Pro account, no strings — want me to send the link?"
- [ ] If they reply, ask to demo Videfy 1:1 (15 min)
- [ ] No affiliate ask yet — let the relationship breathe
- [ ] Goal: 15-20 demos completed by Wk 8

### Phase 3 (Wk 9-12) — Affiliate Activation
**Goal:** Convert demos to active affiliates.

- [ ] After demo, send affiliate offer (30% × 12 months + free Pro)
- [ ] Co-create a piece of content with the affiliate (split outputs)
- [ ] Public shoutout of affiliate's work in newsletter / X
- [ ] Goal: 30+ active affiliates by Wk 12

### Phase 4 (Wk 13-20) — Collaborations
**Goal:** Move from affiliate to collaboration.

- [ ] Joint webinars / live demos with top affiliates
- [ ] Cross-promote each other's products / services
- [ ] Founder podcast appearances on their shows
- [ ] Affiliate-of-the-month featured in newsletter
- [ ] Co-branded content (rare, only top 5 affiliates)

### Phase 5 (Wk 21-26) — Press + Partnerships
**Goal:** Move beyond affiliates to press, agency partnerships, conferences.

- [ ] Pitch press: AI Breakdown, Lenny's, Marketing Brew, This Week in Startups
- [ ] Conference keynote slot booked
- [ ] First strategic partnership (with established AI tool or agency network)

## Feedback loop with influencers

Set up dedicated feedback channel for influencer + affiliate input.

### Options
- **Private Discord** — preferred. Channel for top 20 influencers/affiliates. Founder participates 30 min/day.
- **Slack Connect** — if more enterprise-y
- **Loom video weekly** — founder records 5-min update, sends to top affiliates, asks for input

### What to ask
- "What feature would you want next?"
- "What's confusing about onboarding for your audience?"
- "What's a hook that's working in your niche right now?"
- "Who else should we talk to?"

**Cadence:** weekly check-in (30 min Loom). Monthly 1:1 with each top-5 affiliate.

## "Build the relationships" — concrete tactics

1. **DM after 4+ comments.** Not before.
2. **Personal video DM > written DM.** 30-second Loom. 3x reply rate.
3. **Send free Pro with custom note.** Not bulk.
4. **Tag them in YOUR content** when relevant. They notice.
5. **Show up in their Lives / Spaces** unannounced. Be visible.
6. **Mail something physical.** $20 sticker pack to top 10 affiliates. Returns 100x.
7. **Co-create one piece of content.** Even small. Now they're invested.
