## The 7 Universal Patterns

### 1. Founder personal account beats company account 10x
- Linear's @karri founder, Lovable's @anton, Cluely's Roy Lee.
- Algorithms weight individual humans over corporate logos.
- **Action:** Founder X account live Day -30. Company account secondary.

### 2. Hook discipline > posting discipline
- Roy Lee brainstorms **20+ hooks per sitting**. Filter: "Could this hit 100M views?"
- Posted 1,000+ tweets in 3 months to find what works.
- Rule: "If half audience doesn't hate it, not viral enough."
- **Action:** Generate 30 hook concepts before producing any video. Kill 80%.

### 3. Specificity beats abstraction
- Winning hook: "I spent $50,000 on ads before figuring out what works"
- Losing hook: "Here are some advertising tips:"
- Real numbers, real timeframes, real mistakes, real names.
- **Action:** Every Videfy hook needs a specific dollar / time / name reference.

### 4. Recurring creator economy > one-shot bounty
- Higgsfield $100K/week pool > Cluely $1K viral bonuses > pure organic
- Cluely pays $20-40 per approved video + $1K/million-view bonus
- Targeting 1,000 UGC creators
- **Action:** Skip $10K one-shot. Run $20K/wk recurring pool with tiered payout.

### 5. Built-in viral product mechanic compounds free
- Lovable: every output = shareable URL = live ad
- Cursor: vibe-coded apps that screenshot
- Heygen: avatars in 175 languages = mass shareability
- **Action:** Every Videfy project needs public preview URL + watermark + CTA.

### 6. Build-in-public Twitter is highest-leverage low-cost channel
- Wednesday threads = 3x more engagement than other days
- Arvid Kahl hit $55K MRR with $0 ads, all Twitter
- Educational threads convert highest for SaaS
- Daily replies (15-20 min) compound
- **Action:** Founder posts daily, Wednesday long thread, 10-20 strategic replies/day.

### 7. Press story angle baked into launch
- Devin: "World's first AI software engineer" → 30M views
- Cluely: "Got expelled from Columbia for cheating tool"
- Higgsfield: "$100K/week paid to creators"
- **Action:** Pre-pitch a quotable, specific, polarizing angle. Press needs a story shape.

## Cluely's $20-40/Vid Math (replicable)

If Videfy hires 50 UGC creators @ Cluely rates:
- 50 creators × 4 videos/day × 30 days = 6,000 videos/month
- Pay: $20 × 6,000 = $120K/mo OR $40 × 6,000 = $240K/mo
- Viral bonuses: 5-10 hits × $1K = $5-10K/mo

**At lower bound: $120K/mo for 6,000 monthly videos.** Hits Cluely's 200 videos/day target. Outputs ~10-20M views/month at modest viral hit rate.

If $120K/mo too steep, scale down:
- 20 creators × 3/day = 1,800/mo @ $30 = **$54K/mo** for 60 videos/day
- 10 creators × 2/day = 600/mo @ $30 = **$18K/mo** for 20 videos/day

Even bottom tier beats $10K one-shot bounty by 50x in content volume.

## Linear's Anti-Bomb Model (alternative)

Worth considering if Videfy product isn't ready for mass.

**Mechanic:**
- Invite-only beta for ~1 year
- Cohorts of 10-20 users at a time
- Build-in-public on Twitter as live changelog
- Each cohort fixes one set of problems before next group joins
- Quality > volume

**Result:** $400M valuation in 5 years.

**When it fits Videfy:** if production isn't bulletproof yet, slow invite-only protects the brand from "AI slop" verdict. Linear's founder explicitly chose the term "issue tracking" to filter out the wrong audience.

## Devin's Hero Demo Pattern

- Single video: AI agent planning, browsing docs, writing code, opening a real PR
- Showed real Upwork freelance task completion
- 30M views on launch tweet alone
- Hype → $2B valuation 1 month later
- BUT: backlash on cherry-picking. Internet of Bugs YouTube exposed flaws.

**Lesson:** Hero demo MUST be reproducible. Cherry-picked demos get dunked. Videfy hero film must be a workflow viewers can replicate.

## What This Means for Videfy

### The recommended stack (research-backed)

**Layer 0 — Founder personal X account live Day -30**
- Daily posts, Wednesday long threads
- 10-20 strategic replies/day to AI / video / agency accounts
- Build in public: shipping notes, screenshots, MRR transparency

**Layer 1 — Built-in shareable URL feature**
- Every Videfy project exports to public preview URL
- Watermark + "Built in Videfy" CTA on every preview
- Counter on profile: "X projects shared this week"

**Layer 2 — Recurring creator earn pool ($20K/week to start)**
- $30 per approved video + $1K viral bonus (1M+ views)
- Approval criteria: brand-locked, made in Videfy, tagged @videfy
- Public dashboard showing total paid out
- Scale to $50K/wk if economics work (CAC < $150 paid)

**Layer 3 — Bomb-day announcement**
- Day 0: Hero film + Earn program announce + Product Hunt launch (12:01 AM PST Tuesday)
- Press pre-pitched embargoed: "$20K/wk being distributed for AI video"
- 5 partner creators post live launch day

**Layer 4 — Hackathon sponsorship program**
- Open offer: "How much credit do you need?" to anyone running video AI hackathon
- $100/attendee in Videfy credits + free Pro month
- Targets: agencies, marketing teams, design schools

**Layer 5 — Arvid-style daily Twitter consistency**
- Founder thread Wednesdays (5-10 tweets)
- Daily metric updates (build in public)
- Shipping notes on features
- 15-20 min/day replies

## Monthly Cost Model

**Lean version (~$40K/mo)**
- Founder time: free
- 10 UGC creators × 2/day × 30 = 600 vids @ $30 = $18K
- Built-in viral mechanic: engineering only
- $5K stand-by ad budget for boosting top organic
- $10K hackathon credit pool
- $5K hero film production amortized
- $2K founder content production

**Aggressive version (~$120K/mo)**
- 50 UGC creators × 4/day × 30 = 6,000 vids @ $30 = $90K
- $1K bonuses per viral hit × 5 = $5K
- $15K Meta + LinkedIn paid amplification
- $5K hackathon credits
- $5K founder production stack

**Cluely-tier ($300K/mo)**
- 700+ clippers + content team
- Press team retainer
- Full creator network ops
